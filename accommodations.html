Accommodation

The rooms in the hotel have floor-to-ceiling windows, and it is easy to see the scenery outside the window. There are also balconies in the rooms with open windows for better views and rest. The design of the entire room allows travelers to fully experience the surrounding environment.

There is a lounge area at the entrance of the hotel where you can take a break when you just come back or wait for your fellow travelers. Of course, if you want, you can make some new friends here, share some travel experiences or even travel together later.

In order to experience more on the road, it is difficult to avoid getting up early. You can have a cup of coffee to add some energy to the journey. You can also have a cup of coffee, go to the lounge area and enjoy a lazy time.
