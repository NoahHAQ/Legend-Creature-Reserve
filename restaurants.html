Restaurant

There is a restaurant on site. You can choose to dine indoors in a quiet environment and enjoy the restaurant's speciality which uses edible farmed legend creatures as raw materials, if you are interested

You also can enjoy your meals outdoors while the breeze is blowing. The table surrounds the pool, so you can splash around with friends or family at the same time

Opposite the pier is a small town and the staffs and some bussinessmen live there. You also can go around the town and eat in street restaurants.
