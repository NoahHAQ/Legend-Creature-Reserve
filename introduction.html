Brief

The resort is built in the heart of the Bermuda Triangle. And at first, due to the confidentiality of the research, it has not been marked on the world map until now and visitors need to go to the southern Turks and Caicos Islands to take a ferry to the reserve. The center island, as the largest island, is built as a living and living center and a major hub for inter-island transportation. The hotel offered by the resort is also located on the island, and the staff welcomes visitors at the hotel. During the holiday, passengers can take a ferry to the ther four islands. The ecological environment of each island is specially created and maintained by humans, so that the living creatures can live and reproduce normally, and passengers can experience the activities and living conditions of these strange creatures.
