Recreation

Near the hotel on the same island, we have large beach for visiters to enjoy the seaside activities. You can go anytime, from dust to dawn or even in the evening. It is casual and convenient. Of course, we recommend tourists more to get in touch with special creatures, because this might be the only chance in life.

On each island, we offer slow tour trains for tours on a circuit route, which will show as many as possible about creatures' living environment. Unfortunitely, tourists are not allowed open the door in the middle of routes, because we cannot guarantee that creatures won't hurt tourists and human activities won't have influence on the environment.

To get a better viewsight, we also supply cable car service on each island. On the cable car you will get a clearer view of the overall living environment and you also can choose to let the guide explain in detail, which is free of charge.

Each island has one own museum about the local creature. You might be interested in the history, dicovery, habit, rencent researches etc. Just find your answer here!
